Warning System

The Warning System are the series of punishment to be imposed to the members that violate General Rules and some of the Major Rules.

Before you startâ€‹

tip

  1. Read the Basic Moderation Commands before reading this.

  2. We require moderators to add a warning first, then adding reason to every punishment they impose to a user.

  3. If the offender violated a Major Rule, proceed to muting and If the offender violated Major Rules 2, 4, and 5, proceed to banning

Additionally, there is an expiration for warning, mutes, and bans.

  • 14 days for Warning and Mutes
  • 30 days for Bans (unless said permanently) and Kick

Read more on Policies on Punishment Expiration
