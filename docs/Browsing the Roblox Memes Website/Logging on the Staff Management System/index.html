To provide efficient handling of workflow in the Group, we released the Staff Management System to the Staffs.

warning

The information included in this page are for beta users only!

Information in this page may change over the course of time as we develop the website.

Accessing the Staff Menuâ€‹

Pre-requisitesâ€‹

In order to access the Staff Menu, you'll need the following details:

  1. Email with "@robloxmemes.ml" at the end

Stepsâ€‹

  1. Go to Roblox Memes Staff Login
  2. Enter the details given by your respective Deparment Heads and click "Remember me".
    info

    If you don't have the login details, please contact your department head by emailing them at dprthead@robloxmemes.ml

  3. If the password and email is correct, then, congrats, you're in!

Accessing the Attendanceâ€‹

  1. After you logged-in, go and click the Quick Access on the top.
  2. Click "Clock In/Out"
  3. You should be greated with a Big Clock, a prompt with ID Number, and if it's time in or time out.
  4. Click "Time In" or "Time Out" and enter your ID Number
tip

If you don't know how to see your ID number

  1. Go again to Roblox Memes Staff Login
  2. Enter your details if asked again.
  3. Click "Quick Access" and go to "My Account"
  4. You should see your ID Number in that webpage.
